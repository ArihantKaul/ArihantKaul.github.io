About Us

In JKYOG Toastmasters Gavel Club, we practice our public speaking and leadership skills in a variety of ways. From a round of impromptu speeches in the form of Table Topics, to the prepared speeches given by different members, to the leadership projects taken up by members and club officers, we hone our skills in one of the most useful, yet rare, skills that one can have - communication.
